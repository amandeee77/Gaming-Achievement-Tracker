Why I Built This

Gaming Achievement Tracker is a simple, elegant way for players to log and reflect on their in-game accomplishments—no accounts or logins required. Most trackers feel cluttered or impersonal, so I designed this app to be visually engaging and easy to use.

How It Works

The app uses the RAWG API to fetch game cover art and genre data, stores achievement entries in MongoDB, and presents everything in a clean, responsive interface. Whether you're trophy hunting or just tracking progress, it makes your gaming journey more rewarding.
